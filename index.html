About Me

Hi! My name is Leah McCormick, and I’m a Toronto-based front-end web developer with an insatiable curiosity and a passion for lifelong learning and writing clean, organized code.

For my entire life, I have been a curious person; always asking questions as to how things work and why they work. I followed my interest in guiding inquiring minds by becoming an Elementary School French Teacher. After a few years of teaching, I made some connections in a different industry, and was offered an Operations position with Google. I spend two years leading the Canadian and midwestern US teams within Google Maps’ data collection, and that is where I found myself thinking about the code behind the applications and algorithms I was using on a daily basis. I followed that interest to HackerYou, and after taking their Part-Time Web Development course, I was accepted into the highly-competitive and demanding Web Development Immersive nine-week Bootcamp where I graduated with a full portfolio of projects, an entire collection of skills and tools, and a wealth of relevant development experience.

After I have found my passion and happiness in development, and I'm finally making the things that work.
