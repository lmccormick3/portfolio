About Me

Hi! My name is Leah McCormick, and I’m a Toronto-based front-end web developer with an insatiable curiosity and a passion for lifelong learning and writing clean, organized code.

For my entire life, I have been a curious person; always asking questions as to how things work and why they work. I followed my interest in guiding inquiring minds by becoming an Elementary School French Teacher. After a few years of teaching, I made some connections in a different industry, and was offered an Operations position with Google. I spent two years leading the Canadian and midwestern US teams within Google Maps’ data collection, and that is where I found myself thinking about the code behind the applications and algorithms I was using on a daily basis. I followed that interest to HackerYou, and after taking their Part-Time Web Development course, I was accepted into the highly-competitive and demanding Web Development Immersive nine-week Bootcamp where I graduated with a full portfolio of projects, an entire collection of skills and tools, and a wealth of relevant development experience. It's pretty exciting to finally be making the things that work.

Also, I'm gonna wanna know more about your dog. Or cat. Or bird. Or the chubby squirrel you saw yesterday.

TL;DR: Curious child turns French teacher then ends up working for Google where she likes the technology, so she learns lots about it and can't wait to learn more.
